Our Story

We Do One Thing
— Fresh Fish, Done Right

Samaki House started with a simple frustration: too much "fresh" fish in Nairobi had already spent days on ice before it reached a plate. So we shortened the chain — straight from trusted tilapia farms to your kitchen, usually within hours of your order.

Every fish is gutted and scaled before it leaves us. No mess, no smell, no standing over a sink — just rinse and cook.

Whether it's a Sunday family lunch, a fry-up with friends, or an ugali night that needs saving, there's a pack sized for it.
